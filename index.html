What is TrackEd ?

TrackEd is the app for tracking study time whether you’re in
college, university, or school. It allows quick time tracking for students
(plus pupils and even parents!) and enables real learning
tracking to hold you accountable!

In other words, TrackEd is a study time tracker that supports you
daily with study time management. It’s one of the best time
management apps for university students — a study time counter
with a focus timer in Pomodoro style!

It's a study tracker tool designed to help you accurately track learning
progress. TrackEd utilizes innovative motivation tools such
as 📈 Share Price, 🏆 Medal Standings, and 🔥 Streak.



So what makes TrackEd special?

  • 📊 Beautiful dashboard: View all your study metrics in one place
  • ☁️ Cloud-based: Access your data anywhere, on any device
  • 🤩 Easy to use: Simple, intuitive interface for effortless tracking
